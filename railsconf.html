B'more on Rails is involved with organizing a number of events for RailsConf 2010, which will be held in Baltimore June 7-10 2010.

Follow @bmoreonrails for the most up-to-date information!

Stay with a Local

As part of a larger effort to welcome the Rails community to Baltimore for RailsConf 2010, B'more on Rails has organized a room-share for RailsConf attendees. We have 12 private rooms available to attendees for the duration of the conference. We see it as an opportunity to welcome you to our city and for us to create a few new friendships.

BohConf

BohConf is the official RailsConf 2010 unconference. At BohConf, we're going to get our hands dirty writing code and sharing ideas in an open and free-form environment. This hacking-centric event will include community code drives featuring well known OSS authors, a code retreat, a programming competition, and barcamp-style discussions. It is free and will run alongside RailsConf in the convention center. Everyone is welcome at BohConf! Follow @bohconf.

B'more on Ales

B'more on Rails approved watering holes, from locals who know better than to drink somewhere lame. Follow @bmoreonales.

bmoreonales.com

Ignite RailsConf

Ignite events are lightning talks, where 16 speakers each get 5 minutes to talk about a subject they are passionate about, but with a twist: the speaker's slides are automatically advanced every 15 seconds. Several B'More on Rails members are presenting! Follow @igniterailsconf!

BWI Ride Request

A cab ride from BWI airport to downtown Baltimore can cost more than $20. B'more on Rails members will be standing by on Sunday June 6 and Monday June 7 to provide personalized rides from BWI to your hotel. We're hoping to have a big red van with a ruby painted on the side doing loops all day between BWI and downtown. More details to come - follow @bmoreonrails for updates.

Yankees at Orioles

We're organizing a group to go to the baseball game on Tuesday night - send a message to @pjb3 if you are interested in cheering the O's on!

Baltimore Ambassadors

When you see one of us in a B'More on Rails t-shirt, say hi! Ask us about Baltimore! Whether you need a recommendation for a place to grab a quick beer, or you need to know the best way to get to the other side of town, we're here to help! Look for the insert in your program, and look for us near the exits after the late afternoon sessions!
